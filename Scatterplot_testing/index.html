Nick Chen Miles Ridden by Day of the Week

April 10, 2017 - June 8, 2017

This visualization summarizes my bike rides between April 10, 2017 and June 8, 2017. During this period I did 66 bike rides, many of which were commutes to and from my work. This visualization reports the total number of miles that I rode on each day of the week during this nine week period. The most interesting insight from this visualization is that I did not go for a single bike ride on a Saturday during this period. During these nine weeks, I rode a total of 711 miles.
